The Greatest Jedi Master?

Yoda is one of the most beloved and perhaps most powerful Jedi masters within the Star Wars universe. He appears within the movies, both old and new, the clone wars, and various other Star Wars Lore. This site describes a brief history of his background, some notable battles, and ultimately, his death. Have fun!
